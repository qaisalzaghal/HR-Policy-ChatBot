Cancellation Letter Format with Sample Template

​“Cancellation is the act of cancelling, calling off, or terminating.”

Writing a Cancellation letter means terminating a Contract, Service, Booking, Agreement, or Membership, etc. A cancellation letter is an official, formal, and legally binding document that ensures clear communication to all parties about the decision to cancel or call off. A Letter of Cancellation is an official declaration that the individual or business is unable to continue and therefore, expresses their intent to cancel or terminate the contract.A cancellation letter or cancellation Email is issued to cancel any written agreement/contract you have with the other party. 

A letter of cancellation is a formal way to communicate to the other party that you are cancelling the contract/agreement/subscription or any other written commitment or terms and conditions that you earlier agreed upon. A Letter of Cancellation is written for various reasons, but its primary objective is to notify the other party that we are not willing to continue the relationship or agreement, and to document the cancellation for all legal purposes. It is good to mention the reasons for cancellation for better understanding and further communication. We have added a few cancellation letter sample templates at the end for learning and better understanding about how to write a cancellation letter to cancel the order. 

Cancellation Letter Format

A letter of cancellation comes in a business letter category and must follow the standard. A well-written cancellation letter format typically includes:

  • Header /Sender's Information :Your information, such as name, address, contact information, and phone number, should be mentioned
  • Date:Date of Writing the Letter
  • Recipient Information:Recipient information, such as name, title, company name, address, City, and State, should be included
  • Salutation:The letter should start with a formal greeting and refer
  • Add Subject Line:There should be a clear subject in, short and to the point, e.g., “Cancellation of Contract/service agreement” or whatever you are cancelling
  • Opening Paragraph with Details of Agreement: Mention Contract number, dates, agreement terms, the intention to cancel the contract, and the reason for cancellation
  • Reason for Cancellation:Mention the reasons for cancellation, details of the clause/points, and also a brief about the expectation you had
  • Request for confirmation: Request a written Confirmation of receipt of the cancellation letter and contract cancellation
  • Closing paragraph: Thanks for their assistance and understanding. In case of any other instruction, it should be mentioned here.The tone/language of the cancellation letter must be polite & firm, but professionalism must be maintained throughout. Please refer to the sample template to learn more about the format and its writing.

Agreement / Subscription/, etc.)]

Refer to our Agreement/Contract dated (Mention here the Subject Contract)

I am sorry to inform you that I am formally announcing to cancellation of my(Mention Contract /Membership/Subscription/Service Agreement, etc.) with [Mention Individual or Company Name], effective from [date of Cancellation]. Find below the relevant details about the contract:

  1. Contract/Membership / Subscription/Service Agreement/Product Name:[Insert name]
  2. Reference Number/ Contract / Agreement Number if any: [Insert reference number]
  3. Reason for Cancellation:[mention the reason, such as Financial Reasons, Service Related Issues or Customer Dissatisfaction, Personal Reasons, etc.
  4. Effective Date of Cancellation:[mention date]
  5. Ask for Refund (if applicable):[request to refund, if required]
  6. Request to Return of Items (if applicable): [Add a List of items to be returned]
  7. Request to Clear Dues or Final Payment: [Settlement of final accounts]

I would request you to please accept and confirm the cancellation and let me know if you require any further documents or any steps you want me to follow to ensure the process cancellation is completed.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Thanks & regards,

 

 

[Mention Your Name]

How to Write Cancellation Letter or Email?

  • Note down the date, the organization’s name, and contact information, and use the right cancellation letter format while writing such an important letter. 
  • While writing a letter of cancellation letter to a customer, it should be politely written and look neutral. A notice period is effective before a cancellation order is effective.
  • When writing a cancellation letter to a company, it should be informed that they no longer have any permission for any activity related to your account (e.g., withdrawal of payments, any other services.)
  • A written confirmation email should be received regarding the status of the cancellation order.
  • Mention clearly the legal steps (judicial action) that would be taken if there is misuse after the notice period of cancellation order is over.
  • The letter of cancellation should be printed on good-quality of paper. Also, a handwritten signature, with the sender's address and contact number, should be taken.

Types of Cancellation Letter

There are various types conditions where a cancellation letter is written depending upon the situation, such as:

  1. To Cancel Service Agreements:  A Cancellation letter is mandatory to cancel the order or to terminate any services such as internet subscriptions, phone contracts, or utility services, Agreements, etc.
  2. To Cancel an Agreement /Contracts: A Letter of cancellation is important in case of canceling employment agreements/Appointment letter, lease agreements, partnership deeds, or contracts for any purchases.
  3. To Cancel any kind of Subscriptions:  A letter is required to send for Cancelling magazine subscriptions, club memberships, or recurring donations.
  4. To Cancel Any Booked Reservations: It is required to write a letter to cancel the order of any kind or to cancel hotel bookings, flight reservations, event registrations, or any such appointments etc.

The letter of cancellation should not to lengthy, but all the details such as account numbers, contract dates, and reasons for cancellation should be mentioned to understand the cancellation

Reasons for Writing a Cancellation Letter

Some of the common reasons are mentioned below that may have led to writing a cancellation letter:

  • Financial Reasons: In case of Inability to continue paying for the service because of unforeseen financial situations.
  • Service Related Issues or Customer Dissatisfaction: Where you found Poor service-related issues for quality of service, not meeting expectations, any unethical or unfair trade practices, or customer dissatisfaction with the product/service, etc.
  • Personal / Individual Reasons: At the time of any such Changes in personal circumstances, immediate relocation, or sudden health-related issues.
  • Compliance / Legal Obligations:A Letter of cancellation is important in case you find any breach of contract, violation of any contract clause, or any non-performance-related issues.

Key Takeaways:

  • Purpose: The main objective of a cancellation letter is to formally inform about ending the contract, agreement, subscription, membership, or service.

  • Legal Document: A Cancellation Letter is a legal document that can be used as evidence in case of any dispute.

  • Format: it is important to follow a formal cancellation letter format, i.e., a business letter structure, add sender and recipient details, with subject, and date.

  • Body Content: Mention contract details, reason for cancelling the contract, etc., and request to have confirmation upon.

  • Language or Tone: The letter language must be very professional but soft and polite.

  • Highlight Reasons: It is good to highlight the reasons, such as Poor services, financial reasons, legal non-compliance, etc.

  • Types of cancellation: There are many types of cancellation letters that can be written, like for service cancellations, agreements, membership, subscriptions, reservations, or cancelling a contract, etc.

  • Confirmation: Once you write such a letter, it is good to have a reply or a written confirmation of the cancellation from the other side.

    Frequently Asked Questions of Cancellation Letter

  • Que: What is a cancellation letter?
  • Ans:

    A cancellation letter is a formal document used to terminate an agreement, contract, or service. It should clearly state the cancellation reason and the effective date.

  • Que: Is a cancellation letter legally binding?
  • Ans:

    A cancellation letter can be legally binding if it follows the contract terms.Always check the agreement for notice period and method of communication.

  • Que: What should I include in a cancellation letter?
  • Ans:

    Include the recipient's details, reference number (if any), reason for cancellation, and the end date and keep the tone polite and professional.
